Antony Sherman

ACS founder and director of services Antony Sherman first became interested in the bespoke furniture industry at an early age when he began dedicating his holidays to work part time as a member of the production team at Mark Wilkinson Furniture Limited, Bromham, Wiltshire. In 1999, following the completion of his University Degree, Antony was asked to join Mark Wilkinson Furniture on a full time basis where his training as a surveyor commenced.

Antony made the decision to leave Mark Wilkinson Furniture Limited at the end of 2009 to further his career and to forge his own path as ACS Project Management, his own company set-up to showcase the best of his craftsmanâ€™s skills.
